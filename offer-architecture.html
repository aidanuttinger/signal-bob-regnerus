Chapter 04 · Offer Architecture

SPARK Offer Architecture

Your offers repriced, repackaged, and sequenced to take clients on a journey — not a transaction.

The Client Journey

From stranger to long-term engagement

Right now your offers sit side by side on a website as independent products. A stranger doesn't know where to start. A SPARK Session client doesn't know what comes next. There's no clear path that takes someone from "just found you" to "deeply committed client." The architecture below changes that.

The SPARK Journey

Each step builds trust, proves value, and creates a natural invitation to go deeper.

Offer 1 · Entry
1
SPARK Session™ — Repriced
$1,500 (was $777)
One hour. One insight. One action. The diagnostic that strips away noise and maps the next move. Your entry point — now priced to signal the caliber of work and client you serve. At $777 you attract price-shoppers. At $1,500 you attract people who've already decided they need what you do.
1 hourRemote or in-personImmediate claritySpecific decision required

The price increase is also a positioning signal. A $777 session says "try me." A $1,500 session says "this is serious work." Your best clients — the ones who leave testimonials like Frank Michel's — don't want to "try" a coach. They want to commit to one.

Offer 2 · Flagship
2
SPARK Tee Time™ — Elevated to Flagship
$5,500–$7,500
A full-day private immersive experience. Pre-round clarity call. Breakfast. 18 holes of coaching in motion. Early dinner debrief. Follow-up SPARK Session one week later. Black-car service, two nights, two meals. This is your most differentiated, most defensible product — and it should be the centrepiece of everything you market.
All-inclusive dayChicago or home courseOptional plus-1Pattern resetFollow-up session included

Comparable experiences — high-end leadership retreats, executive offsites, mastermind experiences — charge $5K–$25K for group formats. You're delivering this privately, with a 40-year expert, tailored entirely to the individual. The pricing ceiling here is higher than you've tested. Start at $5,500, move to $7,500 when demand justifies it.

Offer 3 · Ongoing
3
SPARK Retainer — New Offer
$2,500–$4,000/month
Monthly ongoing engagement for leaders who've had a SPARK Session or Tee Time and want to keep the mirror active. Two sessions per month. Async access between sessions. Accountability on the specific pattern identified in their initial work. The pattern doesn't resolve in a day — this offer serves the ones who want to make sure it stays resolved.
2 sessions/monthAsync accountabilityPattern trackingMinimum 3-month commitment

This is predictable recurring revenue — the thing the session model fundamentally can't provide. Four retainer clients at $3,000/month = $144K ARR before a single new SPARK Session or Tee Time booking. This offer should be introduced at the follow-up session included in Tee Time.

Offer 4 · Authority
4
SPARK Keynote — Activate Speaking
$5,000–$20,000
"How you do one thing is how you do everything" as a keynote for executive audiences, leadership conferences, founder summits, and high-performance organizations. Your story — from locker room to boardroom, the CEO and the 7-iron — is a natural 45-minute talk that plants the seed for downstream Tee Time bookings.
45–60 minExecutive audiencesFounder/athlete crossoverDownstream pipeline

Speaking isn't just revenue. It's your most efficient lead generation channel. One keynote to a room of 100 founders will generate more SPARK Session bookings than six months of social media. Prioritise speaking engagements in Year 1 of the activation plan.
