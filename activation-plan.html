Chapter 09 · Activation Plan

90 Days to Fully Broadcast

Week by week — no overwhelm, no guessing. Just the next move, and the one after that.

The North Star

By Day 90 you have: a rebuilt LinkedIn profile generating weekly inbound, a SPARK Insights newsletter with a conversion architecture, a fully priced offer suite, a reactivated warm list with at least 2–3 new bookings from dormant contacts, and one Tee Time confirmed. That's not a fantasy — it's the output of doing the right things in the right sequence.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–2
Foundation: Get the Signal Right
Days 1–14
Rebuild LinkedIn profile — new headline, about section, featured section. Takes 2 hours. Immediate positioning shift.
Update SPARK Session pricing to $1,500 — on the website and in all booking links. No gradual increase. Clean cut.
Pin The Mental Yardage Book as list-builder on LinkedIn, website, and in email signature.
Write first SPARK Insights edition using the new 5-part anatomy (Chapter 07). Publish and send.
Segment your email list — active vs. cold. Tag past clients separately for the reactivation sequence.
Week 2 Milestone
LinkedIn rebuilt. Pricing updated. First structured newsletter sent. List segmented.
Phase 2 · Weeks 3–4
Reactivation: Wake the Warm List
Days 15–28
Send reactivation sequence to past SPARK Session clients — personal check-in, value drop, Tee Time invitation (Chapter 05 templates).
Send newsletter-to-booking CTA to active subscribers — one direct, bounded invitation to book a session this month.
Personal outreach to top 10 referrers — thank them for past referrals, offer a reciprocal session.
Post 3x on LinkedIn this week — Pattern Story Monday, Golf Mirror Wednesday, Conviction Friday. No CTA yet — just building presence.
Design the Retainer offer — one-page description, pricing, commitment structure. Ready to present to right clients at end of sessions.
Week 4 Milestone
Reactivation emails sent. First 1–2 responses received. LinkedIn rhythm established. Retainer offer ready.
Phase 3 · Month 2
Engine: Inbound Starts Working
Days 29–60
Maintain 3x/week LinkedIn posting — content rhythm is now habitual. Start replying to every comment with depth, not thanks.
Introduce Tee Time CTA in one LinkedIn post and one newsletter edition. "Two slots open for spring. This is what happens." Link to Tee Time page.
Book first speaking engagement — EO chapter, founder conference, leadership event. One talk this quarter. Offer it at cost or free in exchange for email capture.
Offer Retainer to best-fit session client — at the end of a SPARK Session where the work is clearly ongoing. Not a pitch — a natural next step.
Ask for 2 testimonials from recent clients. Specific format: "what was the situation before, what shifted, what happened after." Add to website and LinkedIn.
Day 60 Milestone
LinkedIn generating weekly connection requests and DMs. Newsletter list growing. One Tee Time booked or in conversation. Speaking engagement confirmed.
Phase 4 · Month 3
Scale: Compound the Signal
Days 61–90
Review and refine content — which LinkedIn posts got the most resonant engagement? Double down on those story types. Kill what isn't working.
Keynote pitch to 3 events or organizations — use the speaking engagement from Month 2 as proof. Start building a speaking page with video clip if possible.
Activate referral system — every client at end of engagement gets: "If you know one person circling a real decision who'd benefit from this work, I'd love an introduction."
Review pricing — if SPARK Sessions are filling up, raise to $2,000. Scarcity is a natural pricing signal. Don't wait for permission.
Day 90 Milestone
Consistent inbound from LinkedIn. At least 1 retainer client. 2+ Tee Times delivered or booked. Speaking pipeline active. Revenue baseline materially higher than Day 0.
