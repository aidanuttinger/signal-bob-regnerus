About Coach Reg

40 years.
One question.

I started in locker rooms. Competitive athletics, high-level coaching, the kind of environment where pressure doesn't lie — and everyone's trying to hide it anyway. You can't hide your pattern from a golf swing. And I learned that early.

Over 40 years and 1,500 athletes — from amateur golfers to professional competitors to the boardrooms of founders who happen to play — I developed a methodology for finding the pattern running underneath everything. It has a name: S.P.A.R.K. And it works just as well in a boardroom as on the back nine.

"The tighter you grip the club, the worse you play." I've watched this truth reveal itself in golf and business so many times it stopped surprising me. What surprises me is how rarely people are shown it.

I'm an EMCC-accredited coach and a certified Purpose Factor® facilitator. But mostly I'm someone who's spent four decades getting very good at one thing: finding the pattern that's running the show — and helping you decide what to do with it.

I work with a small number of clients at a time. That's intentional. If you're reading this and something is landing, that's worth paying attention to.
