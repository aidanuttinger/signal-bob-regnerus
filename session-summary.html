Chapter 01 · Session Summary

What We Found

The diagnostic. What's working, what's leaking, and the one shift that changes everything for your revenue and reach.

The Signal Read

Bob, your work creates conviction.
Your broadcast doesn't.

You've spent four decades helping athletes and founders see the pattern running underneath their performance. You can spot a white-knuckle grip — on a golf club or a business — within minutes. CEOs leave your SPARK Sessions with clarity they couldn't find in a year of thinking alone.

And yet: the world doesn't know you exist.

This isn't a quality problem. The product is exceptional. The testimonials are extraordinary. The methodology is battle-tested across 1,500 athletes and hundreds of boardroom conversations. What's missing is the broadcast — the systematic, consistent amplification of your signal that turns strangers into clients and clients into advocates.

The pattern you help others break? It's running in your own business. How you do one thing is how you do everything.

What's Working

The strengths we're building on

The SPARK methodology is genuinely differentiated
S.P.A.R.K. is a precise, repeatable framework with a memorable name, clear outcomes, and strong social proof. Michael Hauge, Jhana Li, Frank Michel — these aren't fluff testimonials. They're from people with credibility. That's rare and valuable.
SPARK Tee Time is a category-of-one offer
There is no one else offering a full-day immersive golf coaching experience that addresses business patterns through play. This is defensible, memorable, and premium. It needs to be the centerpiece, not a footnote.
40 years of pattern recognition is an authority moat
1,500+ athletes. Locker rooms to boardrooms. Head golf coach. This backstory positions you as someone who has earned the right to see what others can't. Most coaches are self-appointed. You were forged over decades.
The SPARK Insights newsletter exists
You have a content channel. That's infrastructure most coaches don't build. The question is whether it's converting subscribers into clients — and the answer is: not systematically, yet.
Where It's Leaking

The gaps costing you revenue

$777 is dramatically underpriced for the clients you serve
Frank Michel is a Partner at Brandywine M&A. Jhana Li founded Spyglass Ops. These are people with $50K+ problems. A one-hour session that delivers that level of insight should not cost less than a decent dinner. This is a positioning problem disguised as a pricing problem.
No systematic reactivation of past clients
Every person who's had a SPARK Session is a warm lead for Tee Time. Every Tee Time participant is a warm lead for an ongoing engagement. There's no current system to move people up this ladder or bring back those who've gone quiet.
LinkedIn is an untapped authority channel
Your ideal client — the founder who's outgrown their definition of success — lives on LinkedIn. They're scrolling while half-present at meetings they've already mentally left. Your stories (the CEO with the white-knuckle grip, Paul's burnout breakthrough) are exactly the content that stops them.
SPARK Tee Time is buried and undermarketed
Your highest-value, most differentiated offer is not front and center. Most people who could afford $4,900 for a life-changing day don't know it exists. This offer should be leading, not trailing.
The newsletter doesn't have a clear conversion path
Great content without a call to action is charity. SPARK Insights needs a structure where each edition moves readers one step closer to booking a session — not just enjoying the insight and moving on.
The Core Insight

"The tighter you grip the club, the worse you play." You tell your clients this. Your business has been gripping too tight — holding onto underpriced offers, comfortable invisibility, and a broadcast rhythm that doesn't match the quality of the work. It's time to loosen the grip."

The shift isn't in the product. It's in the broadcast. Your methodology is complete. Your authority is real. What this session delivers is the architecture to transmit it — consistently, systemically, and at the price point your work deserves.

Nine deliverables. Each one designed to amplify a different frequency of your signal. Together, they form the complete revenue and reach architecture for Coach Reg at his next level.
